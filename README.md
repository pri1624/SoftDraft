
# SoftDraft

Softdraft is your creative playground for designing the perfect journal spread before anything becomes final. Upload images, cut and crop elements, experiment with backgrounds, fonts, and even freehand drawing to bring your vision to life. No pressure, no mistakes, just pure creativity.

Journaling can feel intimidating when every choice is permanent, but Softdraft removes that fear. Freely test layouts, move things around, and refine your ideas until they feel just right. Think of it as your safe space to create a soft draft before the final page.
