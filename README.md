# ✨ SoftDraft

**SoftDraft** is your creative playground for designing the perfect journal spread, before anything becomes final.

Upload images, cut and crop elements, experiment with backgrounds, fonts, and even freehand drawing to bring your vision to life. No pressure, no mistakes, just pure creativity.

Journaling can feel intimidating when every choice feels permanent, but SoftDraft removes that fear. Freely test layouts, move things around, and refine your ideas until they feel just right. Think of it as your safe space to create a *soft draft* before the final page.


## 🎨 Features

- 🖼️ Upload and arrange images  
- ✂️ Cut and crop elements  
- 🎨 Customize backgrounds and fonts  
- ✏️ Freehand drawing tools  
- 🧩 Drag-and-drop layout building  
- 🔄 Experiment freely before committing to paper
